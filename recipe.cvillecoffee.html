Coq au Vin from Burgundy

Serves: 8

Ingredients

  • 1 Large chicken, about 5-6 pounds, cut up
  • 3 tablespoons, olive oil
  • 2 onions, minced
  • 3 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 3 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 bouquet garni (bay leaf, parsley, thyme)
  • Pepper and salt
  • 1 cup cognac
  • 1 bottle Burgundy Pinot Noir
  • 1/2 pound slab bacon, cubed
  • 8 ounces white mushrooms, diced
  • 1 cup parsley, finely chopped
  • 1 grand poulet, 5-6 livres
  • 3 grandes cuillères d'huile d'olive
  • 2 oignons
  • 3 carottes
  • 3 gousses d'ail
  • 1 bouquet garnis
  • Poivre et sel
  • 1 tasse de cognac
  • 1 bouteille Pinot noir de Bourgogne
  • 1/2 livre de bacon
  • 8 onces de champignons blancs
  • 1 tasse de persil, haché finement

Directions

In a wide heavy-bottomed pot or Dutch oven,

CUISINER: BROWN chicken in oil on all sides.

RETIRER: REMOVE and reserve. In the same oil, sauté onion for 5 minutes.

AJOUTER: ADD carrots.

REMUER: STIR.

REMETTRE: Return chicken to pot with garlic, bouquet garni, pepper, and salt.

AJOUTER: POUR in cognac and ignite.

AJOUTER: POUR in wine.

REMUER: STIR and cover.

MIJOTER: SIMMER over very low heat for at least 2 hours.

SAUTER: In skillet, SAUTÉ bacon and mushrooms. Stir into chicken. Remove bouquet garni and adjust seasoning.

SERVIR: Sprinkle coq au vin with parsley, and SERVE with flat noodles, or steamed potatoes.

Breton Apple Cake

Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 5 eggs
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups flour
  • 3 cups milk
  • 2 apples, peeled, cored, and sliced
  • 5 oeufs
  • 1/2 tasse de sucre
  • 1 1/4 tasse de farine
  • 3 tasses de lait
  • 2 pommes, pelées, vidées, et en tranches

Directions

BEURRER: BUTTER an ovenproof dish.

PRÉCHAUFFER: PREHEAT oven to 400ºF.

CASSER: In a bowl, BREAK the eggs,

FOUETTER: WHISK in sugar until pale, then

AJOUTER: ADD flour and milk.

VERSER: POUR batter into buttered dish.

PLACER: PLACE apple slices on top.

CUIRE: BAKE 15 minutes.

RÉDUIRE: REDUCE heat to 300ºF and continue baking for 30 minutes or until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean. Optional: Sprinkle with confectioners’ sugar.

SERVIR: SERVE either warm or cold.

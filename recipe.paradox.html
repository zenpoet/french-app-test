Paradox Pastry Recipe

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Makes: 24

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 1/2 sticks butter melted and cooled until warm
  • 1 cup light brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg + 1 egg YOLK
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups chocolate chips
  • 2 tasse de farine
  • 1/2 cuillère à café de sel
  • 1/2 cuillère à café de bicarbonate de soude
  • 1 1/2 plaque de beurre fondu et refroidi avant chaud
  • 1 tasse de cassonade
  • 1/2 tasse de sucre
  • 1 grande l’œuf + 1 jaune d’œuf
  • 2 cuillère à café de vanille
  • 1 1/2 tasse des pépites de chocolat

Directions

  • FOUETTER: WHISK dry ingredients together and set aside.
  • Travailler: In mixing bowl, cream together sugars and melted butter.
  • BATTRE: BEAT in egg, ylik, and vanilla just until combined.
  • AJOUTER: ADD dry ingredients and mix until combined.
  • AJOUTER: ADD chips.
  • ROULER: ROLL dough into 1/4 cup balls.
  • CUIRE: BAKE approximately 15 minutes.

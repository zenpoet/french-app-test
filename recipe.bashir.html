Tarte Aux Abricots et Frangipane

Simplified Apricot Pastry Tart with Almond Cream

Serves: 1-2

Ingredients:

  • 1 puff pastry
  • 10 apricots (you can use canned apricots)
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

   Almond cream:

  • 100 g soft butter (1/2 cup)
  • 100 g ground almonds
  • 100 g of sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pâte feuilletée
  • 10 abricots
  • 1/2 c extrait de vanille




  • 100 g de beurre mou
  • 100 g d'amandes en poudre
  • 100 g de sucre
  • 2 oeufs

Pastry Preparation

PRÉCHAUFFER. Preheat oven to 350 degrees

BATTRE. BEAT the butter and sugar together

AJOUTER. ADD egg yolks and almonds

BATTRE. BEAT the egg whites and add to mixture

ÉTALER. LAY OUT the dough into a pie plate

CUIRE. COOK for 30 minutes at 350 degrees

Pastry Preparation: Beat the butter and sugar together, then add egg yolks and almonds. Next, beat the egg whites and add them to mixture. Pit the apricots and separate the two halves. Roll out the dough into a pie plate, and then, spread with almond cream and the apricot halves. Finally, bake for 30 minutes.

Almond Cream Preparation

BATTRE AU FOUET: WHISK the butter

AJOUTER: ADD almond flour and egg

BATTRE AU FOUET: WHISK egg whites

AJOUTER: ADD to the butter mix

ÉTALER: SPREAD the almond cream and evenly cover with apricot halves

CUIRE: COOK at 350 degrees for 20 minutes

Almond Cream Preparation: Whisk butter until the sugar is light and fluffy. Then, add almond flour and egg. Next, whisk egg whites to form soft peak, then add to butter mix. Before spreading the almond mixture on the puff pastry make sure you are using a fork to prick the surface of the dough. Set puff pastry in a tart mold. Spread the almond cream evenly and cover with apricot halves. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 mins. Enjoy.

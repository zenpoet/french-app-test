Chef Interview at Pomme Restaurant

Geraldine, Pomme Restaurant

Do you speak French?

Yes, I just moved back from France in July. I was born in the states, but lived in France for twenty years. I met my husband in the States, but he's more French than I am. He grew up there.

What is your restaurant French connection?

The connection is my father - he owns the restaurant and I work with him and he has been here in the US for 50 years.

What drew you to the restaurant business?

I have been in the restaurant business all my life. My dad is a master chef in France, so I have always known I wanted to be in that sort of environment. I went to college in New Hampshire for restaurant management, then I went to France for twenty years and then I came back to help my dad.

When did you first consider yourself a chef?

I first considered myself a chef when I was like eight because I used to love to watch my dad - to me it was an art.

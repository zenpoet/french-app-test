Mariebette Café Recipe

Croque Monsieur

Ingredients

Serves: 8 Sandwiches

  • 1 pt whole milk
  • 1 oz flour
  • 1 oz butter
  • 1/8 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp white pepper
  • 8 slices sourdough bread
  • 16 thick slices black forest ham
  • 16 slices gruyere
  • 1 pt de lait entier
  • 1 oz de farine
  • 1 oz de beurre
  • 1/8 tsp noix de Muscade
  • 1/8 tsp de sel
  • 1/8 tsp de poivre blanc
  • 8 tranches de pain au levain
  • 16 tranches épaisses de jambon
  • 16 tranches de gruyère

Directions

PRÉCHAUFFER: PREHEAT oven to 425º.

BOUILLIR: Bring milk to a BOIL in a saucepan. Reduce heat to low and keep hot.

FAIRE FONDRE: In a separate saucepan, MELT butter over medium heat.

AJOUTER: ADD flour and whisk together.

CUIRE: COOK for two minutes without letting the roux gain color.

MÉLANGER: Gradually WHISK in hot milk and increase heat. Add nutmeg, salt and pepper. Bring to a simmer and cook for five minutes until thickened into a béchamel sauce. Remove from heat.

ÉTALER: On a baking sheet, arrange slices of sourdough. SPREAD with a generous amount of béchamel sauce on side facing up. Cover bread with two slices of ham and cover ham with two slices of gruyere.

CUIRE: BAKE in the oven until cheese is completely melted. Serve immediately.

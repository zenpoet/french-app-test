The Chef's Treasure

Joe: Juste une lettre, Just a letter.

Jacques: Une lettre ou un chèque?

Joe: A letter. Definitely not a check.

Marie: Zut alors! (being sarcastic) Maybe you should read only 50% of it and we should read only 10%. What does it say?

Josephine’s voice: Cher Joe. You are the chosen one! Félicitations! Congratulations! Remember that 1789 is the magic number. You have 17 days to interview 8 chefs and find my 9 secret recipes. After that it will be too late. Hurry, once you complete this task, you will receive my treasure. Bonne chance!

Jacques: She’s right. Luck is what we’ll need!

Joe: OK. Let’s do this! Money money money, here we come!

Jacques and Marie: On y va! Let’s go!

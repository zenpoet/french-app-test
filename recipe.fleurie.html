Fleurie's Recipe

CRÈME BRÛLÉE

Ingredients

Serves: 1

  • 1 qt cream
  • 200 g sugar
  • 2 vanilla beans
  • 8 egg yolks
  • 1 quart de crème
  • 200g. de sucre
  • 2 gousses de vanille
  • 8 jaunes d'oeufs

Directions

COUPER: CUT open and scrape vanilla beans.

AJOUTER: ADD cream and bring to simmer.

LAISSER: LET STAND 10-20 minutes.

FOUETTER: WHISK sugar and egg yolks until pale, and slowly incorporate warm cream into yolks.

CUIRE: Pour brûlée into cups and place cups in a water bath to bake at 325 degrees F for 40 min (ensure cups are not completely submerged in the water).

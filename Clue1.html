The Chef's Treasure

Marie: Yeah! We cracked the code!

Jacques: Ouvre la boîte! Ouvre la boîte! OPEN THE BOX ALREADY!

Joe: Patience, patience. Before I open, I want to make sure we split everything equally; 50% for me and 10% for each one of you.

Marie: Quoi? How is that equal? And it doesn’t even make sense. It’s not 100%!

Joe: Stop being so smart and so precise. Ma boîte, mes règles. My box, my rules! Now let’s see how rich I am. (Opening the box) QUOI! RIEN!

Jacques: NADA?

Marie: NOTHING?

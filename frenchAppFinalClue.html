The Chef's Treasure

17 days later...

Knock knock knock!

Joe: Qui est là? Who is there?

Voice: Alix Cinéma.

Joe: A movie maker? (opening the door): Oh c’est vous. It’s you. I thought you were a movie maker, but no, you are just the lawyer who gave me a box with no money!

Alix Cinéma: Maybe you will like me more now (showing keys).

Joe: All right! I am getting a new car! Europe, here I come!

Alix Cinéma: You will be going to Europe all right, but you will be running Great Aunt Josephine’s restaurant. You will be working 6 days a week, 10 hours/day and now that you have collected her secret recipes, you will become the next Famous French chef. These are the keys to Josephine’s treasure. Félicitations!

Joe: QUOI? NONNNNNNNNNNNN!

Paradox Pastry Recipe

Beef Bourguignon

Serves: 1

Ingredients

  • 5 livres de Boeuf
  • 1 bouteille de vin Rouge
  • 2 quart de bouillon de poulet
  • 1 carotte
  • 1 oignon
  • 2 branche des celeris
  • ½ ail
  • Du thym
  • Du poivre
  • De farine
  • Du beurre
  • Un pot

Directions

(Mariner)Marinate Ribs and vegetables in the red wine overnight.

(Egoutter)Strain in the morning and reserve the ribs, red wine and vegetables separately.

(Saupovalror)Dust ribs with flour and season generously with salt and pepper.

(Chuffer) Heat oil in large casserole pot (no lid)

(Sauser)Sear all sides of beef in hot oil, then remove from oil and set aside.

(Déglacer)Brown vegetables in oil. Deglaze pan with red wine and reduce by half.

(Retourner)Return ribs to pan.

(Couvrir)Cover with chicken stock.

(Ajouter)Add Thyme and black peppercorns and bring liquid to a simmer.

(Couvrir)Cover and put in 250 oven for 4-5hrs.

(Enlever)Remove ribs and discard the vegetables, but reserve the braising liquid.

(Réduire)Reduce liquid to sauce consistency. You might want to "mount" the sauce with butter to finish it, but be careful not to let the sauce get so hot that the butter "breaks".

Inheritance

Lawyer: Je suis désolé. I am sorry for your loss. Your great aunt Jo was a wonderful woman. A very generous person, as well as a successful French chef.

Joe: Great aunt Jo???? I thought I was named after my great uncle Joe!

Lawyer: Ha! Non, You were named after your great aunt Josephine. At least, that’s what she told me. She was an amazing lady.

Joe: Merci! Thank you! But I didn’t know her.

Lawyer: She must have known you because she left you her inheritance.
